## Long description

AI Commerce Suite helps Shopify merchants improve catalog SEO and content without leaving Admin.

**What you can do**
- Optimize product titles, descriptions, and meta tags with AI
- Translate product content for new markets
- Generate image alt text and compress featured images
- Improve collection SEO copy
- Build product schema (JSON-LD) for structured data
- Track SEO overview and today’s activity on the Dashboard

**Plans**
- Free: 20 AI actions per day. Browse your catalog and generate Schema anytime.
- Pro ($19/mo): unlimited AI actions and bulk optimize. Billed securely through Shopify.

Nothing is written to your catalog until you review and confirm Apply.
